**Analysis:** The current design, while "clean", likely feels too "flat" or "corporate basic" due to the simple solid colors and standard card layouts. The high contrast (Navy background + White cards) can feel disjointed.

Here are 3 distinct directions to "Upgrade" the design using `ui-ux-pro-max` principles.

## Option 1: **Cosmic Glass (The "Wow" Factor)**
*   **Concept:** Keep the Navy background but replace solid white cards with **Glassmorphism**.
*   **Changes:**
    *   **Cards:** `bg-white/5` with `backdrop-blur-xl` and `border-white/10`.
    *   **Depth:** Use layered glows behind the cards.
    *   **Typography:** White text on glass, Gold accents for selected states.
    *   **Why:** Immediately looks premium and modern. Addresses the "boring" feeling.

## Option 2: **Neo-Brutalist Gold (The "Bold" Factor)**
*   **Concept:** Lean into the Gold/Navy contrast but make it architectural.
*   **Changes:**
    *   **Geometry:** Sharper corners (`rounded-lg` instead of `2xl`).
    *   **Borders:** Thick gold borders on active elements (`border-2`).
    *   **Shadows:** Hard shadows (no blur) offset by 4px.
    *   **Why:** Looks confident and high-end fashion. Very memorable.

## Option 3: **Refined Soft Luxury (The "Apple" Factor)**
*   **Concept:** Keep the "Soft" approach but refine the execution to be less "blocky".
*   **Changes:**
    *   **Background:** Switch to a very light, warm off-white (`bg-slate-50`) instead of Navy, OR separate sections better.
    *   **Cards:** Add "Super-Shadows" (diffused, large spread) + "Subtle Borders".
    *   **Typography:** Scale up headings, use thinner weights for body.
    *   **Why:** Looks cleaner and less "heavy" than the current dark mode look.
